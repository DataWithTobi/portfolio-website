About Me

I’m a data analyst focused on turning messy operational and customer data into clear, decision ready insights using SQL, Power BI, and AI-assisted analysis.

I design clean data models, write validated SQL queries, and build Power BI dashboards that answer real business questions, not vanity metrics. My work spans customer success, e-commerce performance, and logistics operations, with an emphasis on clarity, accuracy, and business relevance.

I actively integrate AI prompt engineering into my workflow to accelerate exploratory analysis, surface patterns faster, and improve insight storytelling. AI supports my thinking, but every insight is validated against the underlying data and business logic.

I follow a structured, end-to-end analytics process: define the business question, validate data in SQL, model relationships correctly, and only then visualize insights in Power BI. This ensures dashboards are reliable, explainable, and decision-ready.

I continuously build and publish new analytics projects across diverse industries, expanding both technical depth and business context on a daily basis.
